The Biluxe10 Affiliate Programme allows registered users to earn commissions by referring new learners to the platform. When someone purchases a course or bundle using your unique referral link, you earn a commission based on your path.

There are two participation paths:

  • Learner Path — Available to enrolled Biluxe10 students. Offers 65% direct commission + 10% passive (2nd level) commissions.
  • Partner Path — Available to promoters who may not be enrolled students. Offers 40% direct commission only.
✅
No investment required to join. Simply sign up, get your unique referral link from your dashboard, and start sharing. Commission is earned automatically when qualifying purchases are made through your link.

Getting started with the Biluxe10 affiliate programme is simple:

01

Sign up at biluxe10.com. Email verification required. Account is free to create.
02
Choose Your Path
Select Learner Path (enroll in a bundle) or Partner Path (promote without enrolling). Your dashboard will reflect your path.
03
Get Your Referral Link
Find your unique referral code in the Affiliate section of your dashboard. Share it via WhatsApp, Instagram, Telegram, or anywhere you like.
04
Earn When They Enroll
When a referred user signs up using your link AND their payment is approved by admin, your commission is automatically credited to your dashboard earnings.
05
Request Withdrawal
Once your balance reaches ₹500+, submit a withdrawal request from your dashboard. Payment is processed within 7 business days.

Commissions are calculated and credited automatically when the admin approves a referred user's payment. Here is the exact flow:

1
User B signs up using User A's referral link
User B's account records referredBy = User A's email ID.
2
User B submits payment (UTR)
Payment is pending admin verification. No commission is calculated yet.
3
Admin approves User B's payment
System checks: Is this a free enrollment? If yes — no commission. If no — commission is calculated.
4
Commission credited to User A
User A's totalEarning, monthlyEarning, weeklyEarning, and dailyEarning are all updated instantly. A transaction record is logged.
5
Passive commission to User A's referrer
If User A was also referred by someone (Learner Path only), that person earns 10% passive commission on User B's sale.
6
Double-processing prevention
commissionProcessed = true is set on User B's record to prevent any duplicate commission credits in future.
🔒
Commission is only triggered once per qualifying sale. Re-approvals, status changes, or admin edits will not re-trigger a commission if it has already been processed.

Biluxe10 admins have the ability to mark certain enrollments as free enrollments. These are special cases where a user is granted access without a commercial transaction taking place.

  • Free enrollments are flagged with freeEnrollment: true in the system
  • No commission is generated for referrals of free enrollment accounts
  • This prevents gaming the system through self-created free accounts
  • Admins can also set commissionEligible: false on any enrollment at their discretion
⚠️
Attempting to abuse free enrollment to generate fake commissions will result in immediate account termination and forfeiture of all earnings.

The following activities are strictly prohibited under the Biluxe10 Affiliate Programme:

  • Self-referrals — Using your own referral link to purchase or creating fake accounts to earn commissions
  • Spam — Sending unsolicited messages, emails, or posts promoting your referral link
  • Misleading advertising — Making false claims about Biluxe10, its courses, or earning potential to drive referrals
  • Discount promises — Offering unauthorised discounts or refunds on behalf of Biluxe10
  • Impersonation — Presenting yourself as an official Biluxe10 representative without written authorisation
  • Paid ads without permission — Running paid advertising campaigns using Biluxe10's brand name without prior written approval
  • Cookie stuffing — Using technical methods to falsely attribute referrals to your account
  • Incentivised clicks — Paying others to click your referral link without making a genuine purchase intent
