Common Questions Answered

Everything you need to know about Biluxe10 platform

What exactly is Biluxe10?

Biluxe10 is India's premium affiliate marketing platform that combines traditional network marketing with modern technology. We offer an intelligent Auto-Upgrade System that automatically moves members to higher packages as they achieve targets, maximizing their earning potential without additional investment.

How does the Auto-Upgrade System work?

Our proprietary Auto-Upgrade System monitors your sales performance and time with the platform. When you achieve predetermined targets (sales volume or time milestones), the system automatically upgrades your package to the next tier. For example, starting at ₹250 can auto-upgrade to ₹2,499 package when targets are met, increasing your commission from 50% to 58%.

What is the minimum investment required?

You can start with just ₹250 for the Discovery Package. This gives you full platform access, basic training, and eligibility for the Auto-Upgrade System. We believe in making financial opportunities accessible to everyone.

How much commission can I earn?

Commission ranges from 50% to 65% based on your package level:

  • ₹250 Package: 50% Commission
  • ₹499 Package: 55% Commission
  • ₹2,499 Package: 58% Commission
  • ₹9,999 Package: 62% Commission
  • ₹23,999 Package: 65% Commission

Is Biluxe10 legal and legitimate?

Yes, Biluxe10 operates as a legitimate affiliate marketing platform. We follow all applicable laws and regulations. Our business model is based on product sales and services, not on recruitment alone. We maintain complete transparency in our operations and commission structure.

How quickly can I withdraw my earnings?

We process withdrawals within 24-48 hours. Most members receive their earnings in their bank account within 1-2 business days. Minimum withdrawal amount is ₹500, and there are no limits on maximum withdrawal.

Do I need prior experience in affiliate marketing?

No prior experience is needed. We provide complete training from basic to advanced levels. Our training modules cover product knowledge, sales techniques, team building, and digital marketing strategies. We also offer ongoing support through webinars and one-on-one mentoring.

Can I upgrade my package manually?

Yes, while our Auto-Upgrade System handles automatic progression, you can also choose to manually upgrade to any package at any time. Manual upgrades immediately provide access to higher commission rates and additional features.
