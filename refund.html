All purchases on Biluxe10 are final once dashboard or course access has been granted. We do not offer refunds, partial refunds, or credits after digital access is provided.

  • This policy applies to all bundles, individual courses, and any paid content on the platform
  • Access is considered "granted" once our admin team approves your UTR and activates your dashboard
  • Changing your mind, not having time to study, or purchasing the wrong bundle are not grounds for a refund
  • Account suspension or termination due to Terms violations does not entitle you to a refund
🚫 No refunds are issued after dashboard access has been granted — under any circumstances except duplicate payment.

Unlike physical products, digital content cannot be "returned" once it has been accessed. Once you view course content:

  • The knowledge and skills gained cannot be erased or returned
  • Course materials, video content, and learning resources have already been consumed
  • Our operational costs (server, admin, support, mentor time) are incurred upon access

This is standard practice across all major digital learning platforms globally. To protect our learners from uninformed purchases, we provide a free 2-minute preview for every single course on the platform — no account or payment required.

💡 Use the free preview before every purchase. It's there to protect you from buying something that doesn't suit you.
