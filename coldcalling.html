Cold Call Master Script

Step-by-step guide for downlines — from pehli ring se closing tak 🎯

📋 Call karne se pehle
📞
Pre-Call Checklist
Yeh sab ready rakho call karne se pehle
▼
📱 Number kahan se mila — note karo (Facebook Ad, WhatsApp Group, Referral)
📝 Customer ka naam pata karo agar ho
🎧 Quiet jagah pe jao, background noise zero
💧 Paani rakh lo, confident smile ke saath baitho
📊 Course prices yaad karo ya saamne rakho
⏰ Best time: 10am–12pm ya 5pm–7pm
💡 Number source bolo agar puche: "Sir, aapka number humne [Facebook/WhatsApp group/referral] ke through mila tha — aapne skills learn karne mein interest dikhaya tha."
🗣️ Call Flow — Step by Step
01
Opening — Pehle 15 Seconds
Pehla impression = sab kuch. Confident aur warm raho.
▼
🧑‍💼 Agent (Tum) "Hello sir/ma'am, kya main [Name] se baat kar raha/rahi hoon?"
👤 Customer "Haan, main hi hoon."
🧑‍💼 Agent "Sir, mera naam [Your Name] hai, main Biluxe10 se bol raha/rahi hoon. Hum students aur working professionals ko high-income skills sikhate hain — jaise Digital Marketing, Graphic Design, Fashion Design, Makeup Artist, Mehndi Design aur aur bhi bahut kuch. Sir, kya aap abhi ek minute baat kar sakte hain?"
📌 Agar busy ho toh:
🕐 Agar bolte hain "Busy hoon"
"Koi baat nahi sir! Kab free rahenge? Main tab call karunga/karungi — sirf 2 minute chahiye."
Callback time note karo aur exactly usi waqt call karo.
✅ Agar bolte hain "Haan"
Seedha Step 2 pe jao. Energy maintain karo!
02
Purpose — Kyun Call Kiya
Confusion hata do, value seedha dikhao.
▼
🧑‍💼 Agent "Sir, aajkal bahut saare students aur professionals chahte hain ki unke paas ek practical skill ho — jisse ya toh career grow kare ya ghar baithe extra income aaye. Isi liye main aapse connect karna chahta/chahti tha — Biluxe10 exactly yahi karta hai, aur bilkul affordable price mein."
🎯 Yeh step short rakhna hai — sirf purpose batao. Pitch abhi mat karo. Pehle customer ko samjho.
03
Qualification — Customer Ko Samjho
Sahi question se sahi pitch milti hai.
▼
❓ Pehla Question — Situation Samjho
"Sir, kya aap abhi padh rahe hain ya kahi kaam kar rahe hain?"
🎓 Agar Student hai
"Aap kaunsa course kar rahe hain? Aur aage kya plan hai — job ya khud ka kuch start karna?"
💼 Agar Working hai
"Sir, kya aap koi new skill seekhna chahenge — career growth ke liye ya side income ke liye?"
👂 Dhyan se suno. Jitna zyada customer bolta hai, utna easy hoga closing. Bolo mat, suno zyada.
04
Pain Point — Unki Problem Dhundo
Log problem solve karne ke liye khareedne mein invest karte hain.
▼
❓ Key Question
"Sir, abhi aapke liye sabse badi challenge kya hai — career mein ya income mein?"
Answer 1 "Koi skill nahi hai mere paas"
✅ Tumhara Reply
"Sir, bilkul sahi baat ki aapne — aur exactly isi wajah se kaafi students Biluxe10 join karte hain. Yahan zero se start karke professional level tak seekh sakte hain."
Answer 2 "Achhi job chahiye"
✅ Tumhara Reply
"Sir, aaj companies degree se zyada practical skills dekhti hain. Agar aapke paas ek industry-relevant skill hai, toh aapka resume automatically strong ho jaata hai."
Answer 3 "Side income chahiye"
✅ Tumhara Reply
"Perfect sir! Hamare bahut saare learners exactly isi reason se join karte hain — skill seekhke freelance karte hain, ghar se kaam karte hain, ya apna kuch shuru karte hain."
05
Biluxe10 Present Karo
Ab platform explain karo — briefly aur clearly.
▼
🧑‍💼 Agent "Sir, Biluxe10 ek aise platform hai jahan aap industry-relevant skills seekh sakte ho — bilkul affordable price mein. Aur kya milta hai?"
🔁 Lifetime Access — ek baar lo, lifetime seekho
📹 Recorded Classes — apni speed se seekho
⏰ Apne time pe — koi pressure nahi
🎓 Certificate milega completion pe
🤝 Support Team — help ke liye 24/7 available
🛠️ Multiple Skills — ek se zyada courses available
🧑‍💼 Agent "Aur abhi special discounted price chal rahi hai — regular se kaafi kam mein."
06
Urgency Create Karo
Limited offer = act now mindset.
▼
🧑‍💼 Agent "Sir, normally yeh courses market mein kaafi zyada price par milte hain. Lekin abhi Biluxe10 pe special offer chal raha hai:"
👗 Fashion Design
₹2,100
💄 Makeup Artist
₹2,300
🌿 Mehndi Design
₹1,999
🔥 Combo Pack
Best Deal
⚡ Price bataane ke baad RUKO mat — seedha Trial Close pe jao. Silence = customer ke dimaag mein decision banata hai.
07
Trial Close — Interest Check Karo
Direct "buy karoge?" mat pucho — smart way use karo.
▼
❌ GALAT "Kya aap khareedna chahoge?"
✅ SAHI "Sir, kaunsa skill aapke liye sabse zyada useful lagta hai abhi?"
✅ SAHI "Sir, Fashion Design ya Makeup Artist — kaunsa aapke goals se match karta hai?"
🧠 Yeh questions assume karte hain ki customer interested hai. Isse customer khud-ba-khud decision ki taraf move karta hai.
⚔️ Objection Handling
🛡️
5 Common Objections — Tode Har Ek Ko
Yeh answers yaad kar lo — practice karo mirror ke saamne
▼
Objection 1 "Not Interested" / "Interested nahi hoon"
"Mujhe interest nahi hai."
✅ Tumhara Reply
"Main samjhata/samajhti hoon sir. Ek quick cheez puchoon — kya aap feel karte hain ki abhi skill ki zaroorat nahi, ya bas timing sahi nahi lag rahi?"
👂 Customer ko baat karne do. Jo woh bolein — usi ko address karo. Mat argue karo.
Objection 2 "Paisa nahi hai"
"Abhi afford nahi kar sakta."
✅ Tumhara Reply
"Bilkul samajh sakta/sakti hoon sir. Isliye hi humne pricing itni affordable rakhi hai — traditional institutes mein yahi course ₹15,000–₹20,000 tak hota hai. Agar yeh skill aapko earning shuru karwa deta hai, toh kya yeh investment worth it nahi hogi?"
💡 Phir ruko — customer ko sochhne do.
Objection 3 "Ghar pe puchna padega / Parents se poochhna hai"
"Mujhe apne parents se puchna hai pehle."
✅ Tumhara Reply
"Bilkul sir, yeh toh bahut achhi baat hai. Ek kaam karo — aap unhe kya batana chahoge? Main aapko woh exact points de deta/deti hoon jo unhe convince karne mein help karein, jaise platform ki credibility, price, aur kya milega."
📋 Benefits explain karo jaise parents sun rahe hain — simple language mein.
Objection 4 "Sochna hai / Time chahiye"
"Main baad mein sochta hoon."
✅ Tumhara Reply
"Sure sir, bilkul. Kya aap mujhe bata sakte hain ki kaunsi cheez sochna chahte hain — course content, price, ya yeh aapko actually kaam aayega ya nahi? Main usi pe exactly help kar sakta/sakti hoon abhi."
🎯 "Baad mein" ko specific concern mein convert karo — tab handle karo.
Objection 5 "Pehle se koi aur course kar raha hoon"
"Main already ek course kar raha hoon kisi aur jagah."
✅ Tumhara Reply
"Wah sir, bahut achhi baat hai — continuous learning bahut important hai! Kaunsa course kar rahe ho? Kyunki Biluxe10 ke courses actually complementary hain — aap dono saath mein kar sakte hain, ya ek complete karke doosra shuru kar sakte hain. Double skill = double opportunity."
🏆 Closing — Deal Seal Karo

Closing Script 🎯

Objections handle hone ke baad — yahan se deal lock hoti hai

🧑‍💼 Agent (Closing Line) "Sir, jo aapne abhi mujhe bataya — usse main honestly feel karta/karti hoon ki yeh course aapke goals ke saath bilkul match karta hai.

Aur abhi jo offer chal rahi hai woh limited time ke liye hai.

Kya main aapko aaj enrollment complete karne mein help karoon?"
💳 Payment Close — Agar Haan Bole "Perfect sir! Main abhi aapko payment details share karta/karti hoon.

Payment ho jaane ke baad mujhe screenshot bhej dena — main turant aapka access activate kar dunga/dungi.

Koi bhi problem ho toh main hoon hi aapke liye. 😊"
⭐ Golden Rules — Hamesha Yaad Rakhna
⭐
Top Agent Rules
Yeh follow karne waale agents sabse zyada close karte hain
▼
😊 Hamesha smile ke saath bolo — phone pe bhi mehsoos hota hai
👂 Zyada suno, kam bolo — 60% suno, 40% bolo
🙅 Argue mat karo — customer ko hamesha validate karo
⚡ Closing ke baad chup raho — jo pehle bole, haara
📞 Follow-up zaroor karo — "Sochne" waale 80% baar dusri call pe close hote hain
🔁 Har call se seekho — jo kaam kiya woh repeat karo
🚀 Sabse important rule: Rejection = redirection hai. Har "no" se "yes" ke paas ja rahe ho. Practice karo, consistent raho, results aayenge.
